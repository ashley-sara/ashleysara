The Challenge

A gem in Palm Desert
hiding in plain sight.

La Bottega had everything that makes a local business thrive — incredible food, a warm atmosphere, loyal regulars, and a catering offering that was genuinely special. What they didn't have was a digital presence that reflected any of it. Their website was outdated, their local SEO was minimal, and their social media wasn't working hard enough to attract new customers or convert catering inquiries.

Cindy needed a true digital partner — someone who could take ownership of the full picture and make every touchpoint work as hard as the business itself.

The Scope

Full-service, fully owned.

This wasn't a single-channel engagement. Over two years I handled every aspect of La Bottega's digital presence — the kind of end-to-end ownership that creates compounding results across all channels simultaneously.

Web Design Local SEO Social Media Management Graphic Design Content Strategy Digital Marketing GoDaddy Website Builder Google Business Profile

01 · Web Design

Website design & build

Designed and built La Bottega's website on GoDaddy Website Builder — creating a warm, Italian-inspired digital home that showcased their food, wine, and catering offering in a way that converted visitors into customers.

02 · SEO

Local SEO strategy

Optimized La Bottega for local search in Palm Desert — keyword research, on-page SEO, Google Business Profile management, and local citation building to make sure they showed up when people searched for coffee, wine, and catering nearby.

03 · Social

Social media management

Managed La Bottega's social media presence end-to-end — content strategy, photography direction, copywriting, and posting cadence — building a consistent brand voice that reflected the shop's warm, Italian character.

04 · Design

Graphic design & brand assets

Created on-brand graphics, promotional materials, and digital assets for menus, events, catering promotions, and seasonal campaigns — maintaining a cohesive visual identity across every touchpoint.
